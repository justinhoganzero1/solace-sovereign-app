Our Story

In a world where technology moves faster than most can keep up, we saw a gap. Brilliant ideas were dying because people lacked the technical skills to bring them to life. Businesses were paying thousands for simple apps. Creators were limited by expensive tools and complex software.

We asked ourselves: What if anyone could build a professional app with just an idea? What if AI could handle the complexity while humans focused on creativity?

SOLACE is the answer.

We've built more than an app - we've created an entire ecosystem. A super app where you can build other apps. An AI assistant that understands your vision and brings it to life. A platform that works offline, speaks 200 languages, and puts enterprise-level tools in everyone's hands.

Most importantly, we built it with purpose. Safety features are always free - because protecting people matters more than profit.
